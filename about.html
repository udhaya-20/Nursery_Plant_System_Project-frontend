About Our Nursery:

We Indians have always worshiped trees as God, and having trees in houses played an eternal part of our lives. Our forefathers have always believed the practice of gifting trees to be auspicious. However, over a period of time, these practices faded. We aim to bring back the practice of growing plants that are both medicinal and decorative, making them accessible to everyone. Go Green Nursery envision a healthier India that can tackle climate change by creating a greener country now. Our goal is to promote a fair food system by providing access to garden space and healthy food, and by educating new gardeners and farmers of all ages..
